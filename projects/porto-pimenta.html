The Brief

A brasserie that doesn't just serve Portuguese food — it tells a Portuguese story.

Porto & Pimenta started as a conversation between two chefs — one trained in Porto, the other in Mumbai — about what happens when you bring Portuguese coastal cooking to Bandra's food scene. Not fusion. Not gimmick. Just honest plates rooted in tradition, made with what grows here.

They needed a brand that could walk the line between old-world warmth and modern Bandra energy. Something that felt like a handwritten letter from Lisbon, but looked at home on Instagram. We built everything — identity, website, social templates, and a menu system that changes with the seasons.
