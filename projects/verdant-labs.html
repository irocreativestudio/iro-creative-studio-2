The Brief

No greenwashing. Just clean, honest design.

Verdant Labs emerged from a team of IISc researchers who had spent six years building grid-scale energy storage technology. They had the science. What they needed was a brand that could tell the story to investors, partners, and the public — without falling into the tired visual language of sustainability marketing.

We stripped everything back. The identity needed to communicate precision, trust, and forward momentum. No leaves. No globe. No gradient green circles. Just a system that was as rigorous as the technology it represented.
